Juice

Juice is the simplest and most versatile dynamic animation engine for JavaScript and jQuery Plugin support. Make animations that fly in seconds. You can even adjust them while they are actively animating.

Juice can save quite a bit of bandwidth transfer, and the processing is light. At < 7k minified, it can offers much more than any standard animated GIF preloader.
